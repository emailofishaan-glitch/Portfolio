The Thinking

Strategy is the
craft.

I believe the best marketing work begins long before execution — in the discipline of understanding what actually moves people, and why. Three principles guide everything I do.

01 — INSIGHT
See what others miss.
The signal is always buried in the noise. Whether it's a VFX pipeline or a brand audit, I look for the structural truth before touching the surface. BCG-trained frameworks. Design thinking. MECE logic. Applied to real brand problems.
02 — STORY
Shape the narrative.
Cinema taught me that story structure is architecture. Every brand has a story worth telling — but most tell it badly. I bring a filmmaker's instinct for tension, pacing, and resolution to brand communication.
03 — EXECUTION
Deliver under pressure.
Leading 100+ crew on deadline-critical productions is the best operations school. I know what it means to be accountable for quality at scale. That's not a soft skill — it's a competitive edge.
